# Mirai

> **Predicting tomorrow's economy using today's invisible signals.**

## What is Mirai?

Mirai is an **Economic Intelligence Platform** that predicts emerging economic stress by analyzing alternative indicators before traditional economic metrics reflect change.

Instead of relying solely on lagging indicators such as GDP, inflation, and unemployment, Mirai combines behavioral, financial, industrial, and geopolitical signals to detect economic shifts at an earlier stage.

The platform aims to provide **explainable predictions**, helping users understand not only *what* may happen, but also *why*.

---

## The Problem

Most economic indicators are reactive rather than predictive.

- GDP is published months after economic activity occurs.
- Inflation reflects changes that have already spread throughout the economy.
- Unemployment often rises after businesses have already begun slowing down.
- Financial markets capture only a portion of the broader economic picture.

As a result, governments, businesses, and individuals often recognize economic stress only after it has become significant.

---

## Our Approach

Mirai identifies early warning signals by combining diverse datasets from multiple domains, including:

- Consumer behavior
- Retail spending patterns
- Commodity prices
- Logistics and supply chain activity
- Labor market dynamics
- Search and internet trends
- Financial market sentiment
- Geopolitical developments
- Manufacturing activity
- Alternative economic indicators

These signals are transformed into interpretable features that power predictive machine learning models.
